About

What is AFlix?

AFlix is a private, invite-only streaming web app. It pulls content from multiple high-quality video servers so you always have a working source — no account walls, no paywalls.

Multiple Servers

Switch between Videasy, VidLink, AutoEmbed, VidFast and more if one source goes down.

Private Access

Invite-only with username and password protection. Your stream, not the internet's.

Up to 4K Quality

Select servers support full 4K HDR streams. Quality automatically adapts to your connection.

Works on Any Device

Phone, tablet, laptop, smart TV browser — AFlix runs anywhere with a modern browser.

Watchlist

Save movies and shows to your personal watchlist and pick up right where you left off.

IPTV Included

Live TV channels built right in. No separate app needed.

Install

Add to Home Screen

AFlix works like a native app when installed — no App Store needed. Follow the steps for your device below.

1

Open in Safari

AFlix must be opened in Safari for the install option to appear. Other browsers (Chrome, Firefox) on iOS do not support this.

2

Tap the Share button

At the bottom of Safari, tap the ⬆ Share icon (the box with an arrow pointing up).

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap ＋ Add to Home Screen. You may need to scroll down to find it.

4

Tap Add

Confirm the name (AFlix) and tap Add in the top right. The app icon will appear on your home screen.

1

Open in Chrome

Open AFlix in Google Chrome on your Android device. Chrome has the best PWA install support.

2

Tap the three-dot menu

Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner of Chrome.

3

Tap "Add to Home screen"

Select Add to Home screen from the dropdown. A banner may also appear automatically at the bottom of the screen — you can tap that instead.

4

Confirm

Tap Add on the prompt. AFlix will appear on your home screen and in your app drawer like a regular app.

1

Open in Chrome or Edge

Open AFlix in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Safari on Mac also works.

2

Look for the install icon

In the browser address bar, look for the ⊕ Install icon on the right side. Click it.

3

Click Install

A small dialog will appear. Click Install and AFlix will open in its own window without browser chrome — just like a native desktop app.
